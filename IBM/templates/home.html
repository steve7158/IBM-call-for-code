About our Project

Flying Robin

Influenced by thr IBM's Initiative to help people under the Curse of Natural disaster. Flying Robin is a drone that does the Realtime mapping for the victims of disaster event and return the coordinate of the analysed area with the type of situation.
The data can be easily posted on a localised map for Admin/ Guard.
This Data can Be used by the Rescue team to think of the most efficient way of providing relief to the victims.
